Steak with creamy peppercorn sauce

Description

There are a handful of truly great sauces in this world that are deceptively simple to make, and this creamy pepper sauce for steak is one of them.

Ingredients

  • 1 sirloin grilling steak (or any fancier type of beef that you can find.)
  • 3 tbsp of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Thyme
  • Brandy or cognac
  • Beef broth or stock
  • Cream
  • Pepper corn

Steps

  1. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper, then let it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Turn the oven to medium high heat, then place your pan on the oven until you see white smoke.
  3. Add olive oil to the pan.
  4. Sear the steak (90 seconds each side if you want medium rare steak).
  5. Add butter, garlic and thyme to the pan.
  6. Tilt pan to spoon garlic butter over the steak to your desired doneness.
  7. Remove your steak from the pan to prepare for the peppercorn sauce.
  8. In the same pan, add your wine, simmmer rapidly for 1 minute until all the alcohol smell is gone.
  9. Add beef stock and simmer rapidly until it reduces by half.
  10. Stir through cream and crushed peppercorns.
  11. Pour your sauce over your steak and enjoy.
