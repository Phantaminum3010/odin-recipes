French Fries

Description

What do I need to say more about this dish? Everyone loves french fries. This is one of the childhood dishes that everyone of us used to ask moms to make every week.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes or 2 big russet potatoes
  • Salt
  • 1 can of canola oil
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Slice the potatoes 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Soak them cold in water for at least an hour or overnight.
  3. Rinse them twice in cold water and pat them completely dry.
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees. Fry them in about 6 batches for 5-6 minutes. Don’t overcrowd them by placing too many in at a time, they won’t be as crispy. Use a slotted spoon and place on a paper towel.
  5. Place them on paper towels and sprinkle immediately with salt. Serve with ketchup!
